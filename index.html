Welcome to the Little Green Larder!

A little bit about us...

We are an eco-friendly/zero waste shop based in Dundee, and one of our main aims is to help reduce plastic pollution. We sell whole foods, dried foods, fresh food, snacks and beauty products along with zero waste alternatives for day to day items. We offer a plastic free shopping experience, with zero waste alternatives to inspire us to create a greener future.

Come along today!

When visiting the shop we encourage you to bring your own jars and containers to fill up on the items available. You can also bring your old washing up liquid and shampoo bottles to refill!. We are very involved in the community and we hope to promote the message of zero-waste living, not just in Dundee, but in the world as a whole.
